Methodology & Data Sources
Fee model: 2024–2025 post-L2 on-chain data. Base case: 75% normal (~$1.50/txn, ~5 Gwei) + 25% elevated (~$5.50/txn, ~20 Gwei) = $2.50 blended. ETH: 150K gas (institutional USDC multi-sig, Gnosis Safe 2-of-3). SOL: 10,000 lamports (base + priority fee). Capital velocity = avg settlement value × Δfinality (14.83 min/txn) × opp cost rate. All figures are estimates; actual costs vary by network conditions and transaction complexity. Verify independently: Etherscan Gas Tracker · Solana Beach · Solana Status
